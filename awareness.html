📚 Awareness & Rights

What is Harassment?

Harassment includes unwanted touching, stalking, abusive messages, threats, or any behavior that makes someone uncomfortable.

Women Rights (India)

IPC 354 – Protection against physical harassment

IPC 509 – Protection against verbal abuse

Right to file complaint at any police station

Safety Tips

• Trust your instincts

• Share live location with trusted people

• Speak up and report early

• Keep emergency contacts ready
